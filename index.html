List of Lilo & Stitch characters

★ Stitch ★

Character Information

Disney's Lilo & Stitch movie features a fictional character named Stitch, who is also known as Experiment 626. His human best friend Lilo Pelekai is the other main protagonist of the franchise. He is a genetically altered, extraterrestrial life-form (alien) that resembles a blue koala.

Character Information

One of the two primary protagonists of Lilo & Stitch, as well as the best friend of Stitch, Lilo Pelekai. After their parents passed away, she and her sister, Nani Pelekai's younger sister, became orphans.

★ Pleakley ★

Character Information

One of the main characters in the Lilo & Stitch series is Agent Wendy Pleakley. Pleakley was a former Galactic Federation member who was coerced into joining Jumba Jookiba's team in order to help him apprehend the runaway Experiment 626.

★ Gantu ★

Character Information
